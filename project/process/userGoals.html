-- User Goals --

· Impression of Hangzhou ·

- How will your users understand the content of the project?

Since this web application is build as tour guide and trip planner, it should be readable for people of varying reading level. We would use pictures and video to replace the long text descriptions.

- How will your users understand the primary objectives?

This application is aimed to help people learn more about Hangzhou's culture and history. User could also create their own trip plan by using this application; therefore, we would use pictures and tooltips to make a bbrief introduction about this city.

- What content and features do your users need, and in what form do they need it?

User would need a map to locate each viewpoints and themselves.

- How do your users think your interactive projects should work?

User might have a similar experience when they visit the page of lonely planet.

- what are their experience levels with the type of media you are creating?

Users could find out more specific details such as the story behind each viewpoint and folk customs about Hangzhou.
